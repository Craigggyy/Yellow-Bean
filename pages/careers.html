Open Positions

We're always looking for passionate individuals to join the Yellow Bean family.

Full-Time
Barista / All-Around Crew
Tulo, Taal, Batangas
☕

Prepare and serve coffee beverages and food items, assist in kitchen operations, maintain cleanliness, and deliver excellent customer service. Willingness to multi-task is a must!

Qualifications:
  • At least a high school graduate
  • Friendly, outgoing, and customer-service oriented
  • Willing to be trained (no prior experience required)
  • Physically fit and able to work on their feet
  • Trustworthy, reliable, and hardworking
Part-Time
Delivery Rider
Tulo, Taal, Batangas
🛵

Deliver Yellow Bean orders accurately and on time to customers in the service area. Must be responsible, organized, and have good knowledge of local roads.

Qualifications:
  • Must have own motorcycle and valid driver's license
  • Familiar with Taal and surrounding areas
  • Responsible, punctual, and customer-friendly
  • At least 18 years old
Full-Time
Kitchen / Food Prep Staff
Tulo, Taal, Batangas
🍳

Prepare food items according to Yellow Bean's standards, maintain kitchen hygiene and proper food handling, and assist with supply inventory and ordering.

Qualifications:
  • Basic cooking skills required; formal training is a plus
  • Knowledge of food safety and hygiene practices
  • Can work efficiently in a fast-paced kitchen
  • Team player and detail-oriented
Part-Time
Social Media & Online Orders Staff
Tulo, Taal, Batangas / Remote
📱

Manage Yellow Bean's social media accounts, create engaging content, monitor online orders, respond to customer messages, and assist in digital marketing activities.

Qualifications:
  • Proficient in Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok
  • Basic photo editing skills (Canva, etc.)
  • Creative, communicative, and organized
  • Experience in social media management is a plus
