Our Story

From Café Hopping to Café Owning

Yellow Bean was born from a simple yet meaningful tradition — Cashmere Kyra Montenegro and her father would spend their free time café hopping around Batangas, savoring different flavors and atmospheres.

Those cherished moments sparked a dream: "Why not build one of our own?" In October 2023, that dream became reality when Yellow Bean officially opened its doors in Tulo, Taal, Batangas.

What started as a heartfelt idea rooted in family and coffee has blossomed into a community café — warm, welcoming, and dedicated to making every customer feel at home. Today, Yellow Bean continues to grow, driven by passion, purpose, and a love for great food and drink.

Our Journey

Milestones

October 2023
Grand Opening

Yellow Bean officially opened its doors in Tulo, Taal, Batangas. A dream turned into a cozy, sunny café space ready to serve the community.

Late 2023
First Community Appearance

Yellow Bean made its first appearance at the Family Day of Casa San Domenico School, bringing smiles and great drinks to the community event.

2024
Basketball League Sponsor

Yellow Bean became a regular presence at basketball leagues in Tulo, Taal, Batangas — fueling players and fans with energizing drinks and snacks.

2024 – Present
Growing Online Presence

Yellow Bean expanded its reach through Foodpanda and active social media engagement — building a loyal customer base beyond the café walls.

The Future
More Branches on the Horizon

With a growing community of loyal customers, the vision of opening new branches in nearby areas is getting closer every day.

Goals & Objectives

Goals
  • To grow Yellow Bean into a well-loved café in the community
  • To increase daily sales and customer visits
  • To build a strong and loyal customer base
  • To expand visibility through online platforms like Foodpanda and social media
  • To eventually open more branches in nearby areas
Objectives
  • Maintain consistent quality in drinks and food every day
  • Provide fast, friendly, and welcoming customer service
  • Post regularly on social media to engage customers
  • Offer promos and discounts to attract new customers
  • Keep the café clean, cozy, and Instagram-worthy
  • Monitor customer feedback and continuously improve
