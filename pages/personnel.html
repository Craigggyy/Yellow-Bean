Cashmere Kyra Montenegro

Business Administration Graduate

Cashmere Kyra Montenegro is a Business Administration graduate with a strong background in finance, research, and operations. She has experience working as a Research Associate, Associate Accountant, and Treasury Manager, developing expertise in financial management, data analysis, and customer relations.

As the owner of Yellow Bean, she oversees all operations — from product development and quality control to customer experience and marketing. She ensures every drink and dish meets the brand's standards while managing finances, leading the team, and driving growth through strategic decisions and innovation.
