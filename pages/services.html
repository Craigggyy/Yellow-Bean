How Yellow Bean Serves You

Four ways to experience the warmth and flavor of Yellow Bean.

🍽️
Service 1

Dine In

Step into our cozy, Instagram-worthy café and enjoy your meal in a warm, welcoming environment. Our café space is designed for comfort — whether you're catching up with friends, studying, working, or simply unwinding with a great cup of coffee.

  • Comfortable and aesthetic café seating
  • Air-conditioned, clean, and well-maintained space
  • Full menu available for dine-in orders
  • Fast and friendly table service
  • Perfect for solo visits, group hangouts, or quick work sessions
📦
Service 2

Takeout

In a hurry? No problem! Grab your Yellow Bean favorites on the go. Our takeout service ensures your order is packed neatly and ready when you are — hot coffee, fresh food, no waiting around.

  • Quick and efficient order processing
  • Secure, spill-proof packaging
  • Full menu available for takeout
  • Walk-in orders accepted anytime during operating hours
🛵
Service 3

Delivery

Can't make it to the café? We've got you covered. Yellow Bean is now available on Foodpanda, bringing your favorite drinks and meals right to your doorstep. Order from the comfort of your home and enjoy the Yellow Bean experience anywhere.

  • Available on Foodpanda
  • Fast and reliable delivery within service area
  • Securely packed to maintain quality during transit
  • Real-time order tracking via the app

Service 4

Event Cart Packages

Bring the Yellow Bean experience to your special events! Our event cart service is perfect for school gatherings, barangay events, sports leagues, company parties, and more. We set up a fully functional café cart at your venue, serving drinks and snacks to your guests.

  • Available for school events (e.g., Family Days like Casa San Domenico)
  • Sports events and community leagues (e.g., Basketball in Tulo, Taal)
  • Birthday parties, reunions, and corporate events
  • Customizable menu selection for the event
  • Fully staffed and self-contained cart setup
