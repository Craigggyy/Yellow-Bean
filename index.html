Our Story

A Café Born from Love & Coffee

It all started with a simple joy — cafe hopping with her father. Cashmere Kyra Montenegro turned those cherished memories into something real. In October 2023, Yellow Bean opened its doors in Talo, Taal, Batangas, with one clear purpose: to bring comfort, happiness, and great coffee to every customer.

From a small beginning with a heart full of warmth, Yellow Bean has grown into a beloved community café — present at local family days, basketball leagues, and everyday moments that matter.
